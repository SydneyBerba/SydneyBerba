Sydney Venice Berba is an aspiring lawyer who is currently taking BS Accountancy in the University of Santo Tomas. She started studying in UST in 2019 as a Humanities and Social Sciences Student.

Personality

Due to her upbringing, Sydney became an inquisitive and curious person who always seeks to understand more about the world, especially in fields she is unfamiliar with. As a result, she took up BS Accountancy, although she was a graduate of the Humanities and Social Sciences strand.

Furthermore, she is detail-oriented as she strives to make the best output in every project she participates in. Her go-getter personality enabled her to get opportunities to participate in multiple organizations and competitions.

Lastly, she is a logical person who continues to balance her everyday life by creating routines. This is to ensure that every aspect–whether at home, school, or other settings, she is able to allocate wisely her time and effort. Her constant management and planning of activities enable her to truly be present in everything she participates in.

Home Life

As her utmost priority in life, Sydney greatly prioritizes her responsibilities as the second eldest of four children. Not only does she try to help in the daily household chores, but also in the planning of activities such as travel itineraries in order to have a closer bond with them. Hence, she has a strong relationship with her siblings and parents.
